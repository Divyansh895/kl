Campus Life

School Library

Sir Francis Bacon rightly said, “Some books are to be tasted, others to be swallowed, and some few to be chewed and digested”.

The Library being a part of the curriculum has become an important tool in the propagation of academic advancement. K. L. International School Library has four libraries stocked with ample reference material to inculcate love for reading. Our librarians and teachers encourage the children to choose books according to their taste. What cannot be completed in school can be at home. We find that our children are improving not only in the subject matter chosen for reading, but also in language, expression, vocabulary and fluency.

school achievements

Young Science genius stands proud in NSEJS
“Destiny is not created by the shoes we wear but by the steps we take”.

The rewarding hard work of our young science enthusiast Aryan Gupta class 9 was marked loud on records as he qualified for the NSEJS examination, the toughest science exam on higher Secondary level Globally. National Standard Examination in Junior Science is a segmented science exam organised by the Indian Association of Physics teacher in association with Homi Bhabha Centre for Science Education. Standing proud, he is the only child in the district to qualify for the exam participating among 310 students from the entire country. Culminated into outstanding performance the proud prodigy was congratulated by the School management and Principal, Mr. S. Shekhar and bestowed their best wishes.
